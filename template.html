📧 Useful Language · Stage 7 Write a Professional Message
Your Task Write a short email to your manager about a recent recruitment process. Use past simple throughout. Aim for 60–100 words.
Word bank:
received reviewed shortlisted interviewed went well didn't expect
Words: 0 / 60–100
💡 Suggested Answer
Hi María,

I wanted to update you on the recruitment process for the HR Coordinator role.

We received 45 applications last week. I didn't expect so many! I reviewed them carefully and shortlisted six candidates. The interviews went very well — three candidates performed really strongly.

I'll send you the final recommendation today.

Best regards, Paula
