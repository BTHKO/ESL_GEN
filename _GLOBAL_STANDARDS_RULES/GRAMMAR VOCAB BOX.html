Grammar Note
Past Simple — Regular Verbs
Affirmative: subject + verb + ed → She started in 2020.
Negative: subject + didn't + base verb → He didn't attend.
Question: Did + subject + base verb? → Did she apply?
Maria graduated from university in 2018 and applied for her first HR role.
Acciones completadas en un momento específico del pasado.
⚠ Common Mistake
I didn't worked in HR.  →  I didn't work in HR.
No usar -ed después de didn't.
